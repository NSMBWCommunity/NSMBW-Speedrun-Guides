# World 5 

World 5 is arguable the most difficult world in Any%. It contains the any% "gaunlet"- 5-3, 5-Tower, and 5-4. Keeping propellor is vital through these levels. Starting out safe and getting confident with consistent movement will be key to learning these levels.

## World 5 split goals (5-Cannon fadeout) to aim for:

**Beginner:** 12:20 - 12:30

**Intermediate:** 12:10

**Expert:** 11:58 - 12:04

**World Record:** 11:55 - 11:57
