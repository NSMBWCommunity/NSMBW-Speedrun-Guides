# 5-3 / 5-2
For new runners 5-2 is a good "safer" alternative to 5-3 at the cost of a bit of time and the possibility of a Propeller Backup in 5-3

5-3 has a roulette block that has a backup propeller and loses about 10s, and with practice 5-3 easily saves time over 5-2.
